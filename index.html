Welcome to Hexapod Project

Jadavpur University Mechatronics Club's entry for RoboFest Gujarat 2025

Project Overview

Our hexapod robot project aims to create an advanced walking robot with six legs, capable of complex movements and terrain navigation. The project combines expertise from software, electronics, and hardware teams to deliver a competitive entry for RoboFest Gujarat 2025.

Software Team

Responsible for motion control, path planning, and system integration.

Electronics Team

Handles circuit design, sensor integration, and power management.

Hardware Team

Manages mechanical design, prototyping, and assembly.
